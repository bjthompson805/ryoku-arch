Notes that cost several passes to learn:
- Lead with the MEDIUM. "greek marble bust" makes flux render a 3d sculpture no
  matter what style words follow; "manga panel art ... a greek statue head"
  draws one. Never say "marble".
- linocut / stencil / screenprint prompts frame the art on white paper. The
  no-paper-border clause is not optional for edge-to-edge UI art.
- "no text" is weak: stencil prompts still produced "bnjemi" and "2017".
  Check output before shipping.
- Verify, do not eyeball: measure chromatic pixels and the midtone fraction.
  The reference (Berserk) sits at 0.00% colour / 69% near-black / 12.5%
  near-white / 18% mid. A 3d render lands near 25% mid. Above ~20% mid means
  it is being rendered, not drawn.
- fal-ai/flux/dev, ~4s for 2 images at 1024px. Key: ~/.config/fal/key.
